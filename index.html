John williams music:



John williams has created many different compositions, all different unlike many other composers. One reason for this is because he used to be a jazz pianist. This makes him different from composers like Hans Zimmer.



Songs by John williams are always great. I enjoy his star wars ones the most because I can relate to that as a star wars fan. Here are some that I like a lot.


Binary Sunset is really calming tune.


The imperial march is a classic. Extremely original tune, great incorporation of the flute and violins in the middle, just amazing.


Love Pledge and the Arena is a very long song, but the orchestra goes everywhere. The beggining is just like the calm before the storm. 2:40 to 6:30 is a really underrated march. The rest is litterally the orchestra showing how great they are.


Duel of the fates. GREAT composition. The cellos have a really nice touch. A choir carries the melody, which is extremely different. Lots of parts make you think its over, but it starts going again.


The spark. Even if the sequels were garbage this song was really good. It's really great as it shows great buildup to something greater and uses a leigtmotif way back in history.


There are so much more but those are my highlights



Kevin Kiner music:



Kevin Kiner's music consists mainly of the clone wars tv show, which is 7 seasons long and has over 100 episodes. IF you listen to his music without the context, you won't be able to enjoy it to its fullest.


The clones leigtmotif. This leigtmotif is a great one as it shows how the jedi and the clones are brothers in war.


The bad batch's theme. It shows the strength of the bad batch and the skill they hold.


Victory and death. The sadness of the clones' betrayal is put in full force with this song.


As Kevin Kiner has scored more than john williams, for me they don't stand out as much. Also I really don't want to do anymore so :P
